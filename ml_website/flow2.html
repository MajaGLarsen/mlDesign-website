Task description

Build your own portfolio

You need to build your own online portfolio with HTML5 and CSS3 use your prototype from Adobe XD Click here to see. Maybe you'll come close to the prototype, maybe it takes and unexpected creative turn.

You need to produce an online version and document your code and project structure on Github. We expect:

  • An online version of your portfolio on your webhotel
  • A repository for your portfolio project
  • A fitting project structure
  • A validated code

Further more you need to consider the following, when you design your website

  • who's going to see your website?
  • How do I present myself in the best way?
  • How do I show what I have learned so far, and what do i hope to learn in the future
  • What visual style and atmosphere do I want in my portfolio
  • How do I give my portfolio a personal touch
